# Image generation prompts

Mode: built-in `imagegen`

## `classroom-speaking.webp`

```text
Use case: stylized-concept
Asset type: landing page section image
Primary request: A joyful Vietnamese children's English conversation practice in a live Zoom-style small-group online class with no more than six children.
Scene/backdrop: warm modern Vietnamese home-study spaces represented as a clean six-tile video-call composition, subtle educational shapes in the background, no readable interface text.
Subject: six Vietnamese children aged roughly 7–12, diverse boys and girls, visibly engaged and taking turns speaking English; natural happy expressions, one child confidently speaking while the others listen and react; an approachable Vietnamese female English teacher facilitating.
Style/medium: premium soft 3D editorial illustration, polished modern education campaign visual, rounded forms, refined rather than childish, consistent character proportions and facial quality.
Composition/framing: wide landscape website section image, balanced group composition, faces and hand gestures clearly visible, generous clean margins, uncluttered.
Lighting/mood: bright soft daylight, optimistic, warm, safe, energetic, trustworthy to parents.
Color palette: dominant sky blue #0EA5E9, clean pale-blue and white background, warm orange #F97316 accents used sparingly in clothing and learning objects.
Materials/textures: soft matte 3D surfaces, subtle fabric texture, gentle realistic depth and shadows.
Constraints: exactly six children maximum plus one teacher; clearly Vietnamese children; online small-group class context; no text, no letters, no logos, no watermark, no brand marks, no legible Zoom UI; anatomically natural hands and faces.
Avoid: crowded classroom, physical school room, generic Western children, babyish cartoon style, neon colors, oversaturated gradients, visual clutter, distorted hands, duplicated faces.
```
